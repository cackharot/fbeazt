About us

Who is foodbeazt Pondicherry?

We are a small team of problem solvers, designers, thinkers and tinkers, working around the clock to make foodbeazt the most powerful online and offline tool for food delivery in Pondicherry. We believe that ordering food should be easy, fast and definitely fun! We wanted something simpler, so we made it.

Foodbeazt Food Delivery

Staying in the office for lunch? Tired of the same takeaways? No more wondering what is available near you. foodbeazt has offline menus from the staggering selection of delivery restaurants around you. Simply call us and make an order by providing the information like cuisine type, restaurants or even price range. We will provide you the restaurant index also includes address and delivery hours. No online or offline food delivery is too difficult for foodbeazt! Food delivery service has never been made easier! Do you have any questions? Let us know!

How it works

Our Services

Order by Call

We receive orders through phone call (73 73 73 04 84 / 81) to delivery your favorite food from your favorite restaurant / Hotels / Fastfood to you.

Home Delivery

We deliver your favorite foods at your door step with free delivery charge for the order above 200 otherwise we charge the minimum delivery fee as Rs. 20/-

Cash on Delivery

With Cash on Delivery you can pay in cash at the time of actual delivery of the product at your doorstep, without requiring you to make any advance payment online.
